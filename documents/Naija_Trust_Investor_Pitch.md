## 1. The Problem: The Trust Deficit
**"Trust is the currency of commerce, but in Nigeria, inflation has hit trust hard."**

*   **For Consumers**: Fear of "What I Ordered vs. What I Got" scams prevents millions from shopping online or trying new service providers.
*   **For Businesses**: Legitimate SMEs struggle to differentiate themselves from scammers. Good businesses die because they cannot prove their credibility to new customers.
*   **The Gap**: Instagram comments are messy, Twitter (X) dragging is chaotic, and Google Reviews lacks local verification context.

---

## 2. The Solution: Naija Trust
**"A Centralized Verification & Reputation Platform for Nigeria."**

Naija Trust is the "Yelp + Better Business Bureau" for the African market.

*   **Verified Identity**: We don't just list businesses; we verify them. Consumers see a "Green Badge" and know this business exists and is registered.
*   **Two-Way Resolution**: Unlike social media mob justice, we provide a structured channel for businesses to reply to reviews and resolve disputes professionally.
*   **Data-Driven**: We give businesses analytics on who is viewing their profile, turning reputation into actionable insights.
