Pastor Solomon Apreala and Pastor Augusta Apreala serve as the Senior Pastors of Peniel Assembly, where they are widely respected as grounded and passionate teachers of the Word of God. Their ministry is marked by depth, clarity, and a strong commitment to sound biblical doctrine that transforms lives.

Beyond the pulpit, their lives are a compelling testimony of godly living in action. What they teach is consistently reflected in how they live through humility, integrity, love, and service. Their faith is not merely expressed in words but demonstrated daily through their deeds, relationships, and leadership.

Married for over ten years, Pastor Solomon and Pastor Augusta share a strong, Christ-centred union and are blessed with two sons. Together, they model a balanced Christian life that harmoniously integrates ministry, marriage, family, and purpose. Their journey continues to inspire many to pursue an authentic walk with God, rooted in truth, grace, and unwavering devotion.
